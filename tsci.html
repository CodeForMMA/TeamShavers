Team Shavers:
Where It All Begun

It is September 1993, Florida. The clanning world was dying. The sky dark and roaring with thunder, amid the bellowing of the sky a child is born, his arrival setting tone to a new era of clanning. An Era like no other.

By the time he was 11 he had mastered the art of tanking, by 13 he was regarded as one of the most influential members of the clanning world. The mentioning of his name sent hundreds fleeing.

Aged 19 he put together the finest members of the clanning community to begin what the clanning community still regard, to this day, as the best clan to have ever entered the multi scene. with spams shrouding skies.

Team Shavers was born.
